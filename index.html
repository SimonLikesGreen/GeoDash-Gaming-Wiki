What is the "Gd Gaming" Community?

The GD Gaming Community are a group of tiktokers that post any gameplay of one GD level with some twists lots of times, this community started with Clutterfunk Gaming.
This Wiki Explains it all! The Wiki updates mostly updates every Saturday (Filipino Time) you may also check the GGMDL (GD Gaming "Most Demons" Leaderboard).

Types of Gaming Videos:

    Non Edited (First Video)

    • This is usually the first video for most of the GD Gaming Community, the first non-edited video shows the level,
      introducing the watcher/doomscrolling the level you will post from now on.

    Edited (Video Editor)

    • These videos are made using Video Editor's, like Capcut, examples of these are when the gaming account has a daily schedule and is out of ideas.

    Level Edited

    • These videos are made from Recordings from the a level edited on the GD level editor, These levels usually are higher quality then ones made on Video Editors.
